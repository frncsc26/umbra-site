Drone Engine

Up to 6 oscillators with independent detune, brightness blend, and slow LFO drift. From pure sine to full sawtooth with one knob.

🎹

Arpeggiator

Scale-aware arp with swing, ping-pong stereo, per-note attack envelope, and four waveforms. Separate from the drone layer.

✂️

Fragment

16-step rhythmic gate that cuts and granularizes the output. Six patterns, adjustable depth and grain — from stutter to glitch.

🌊

Echo + Reverb

Echo placed before reverb so repeats swim in space. Large hall reverb for endless depth. Separate delay for rhythmic trails.

🎛️

9 Presets

From bright Lydian drones to dark tension scales. Phosphene, Debris Cloud, Motorik and more — each with its own character.

⚡

Real-time

Allocation-free audio thread, spin-lock parameter passing, all controls automatable in real time without clicks or dropouts.

How to install

01 Download the Umbra.dmg file below
02 Open the DMG and drag Umbra into your Applications folder
03 First launch: right-click → Open (instead of double-click). macOS will ask for confirmation — click Open. Normal double-click works from then on.
